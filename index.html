Got Questions? We Have Answers!

Wondering how to get started on building your dream home? We've answered the most common questions below. If you need more help, don’t hesitate to contact us!

Some FAQ

What kind of construction do you guys specialize in?

We specialize in residential construction, focusing on designing and building custom homes that meet our clients' unique needs and preferences. Our expertise includes everything from modern urban residences to traditional countryside homes, with an emphasis on quality, innovation, and sustainability in every project.

How long does a typical construction project take?

Project timelines can vary depending on the complexity and size of the home. Generally, a typical residential construction project takes between 6 to 12 months from initial design to completion. We work closely with you to establish a detailed schedule and keep you updated throughout the process to ensure timely delivery.

How do you charge for projects? By the hour, job, etc.?

We typically charge based on the scope and complexity of the project. This can include a fixed fee for the entire job, a percentage of the total construction cost, or a combination of both. We provide a detailed estimate after discussing your project requirements and ensure transparency throughout the process. Contact us to get a customized quote for your specific needs

Who is responsible for getting the necessary permits?

We handle the process of obtaining all necessary permits for your project. Our team ensures that all required approvals and inspections are completed, so you don’t have to worry about navigating the regulatory aspects. We keep you informed throughout the process to ensure everything is in compliance with local building codes and regulations

Do you offer free in-person estimates?

Yes, we offer free in-person estimates to discuss your project in detail. This allows us to understand your vision, assess the scope of work, and provide an accurate estimate tailored to your needs. Contact us to schedule a convenient time for your free consultation.

What is your service area?

We primarily serve [City/Region], but we are also available for projects in surrounding areas. If you’re located outside our main service area, please reach out to us to discuss your project—we may be able to accommodate your needs
